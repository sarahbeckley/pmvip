Do you struggle with depression or anxiety?
Before: When I was depressed...
Now: That I know what to do...

I'm Patrick Morales, founder of PM VIP Fitness, and I'm here to tell you that there is hope when struggling with depression and anxiety. I am an ACE certified personal trainer that has experienced severe depression and have used physical exercise, nutrition and mindset to overcome this dehabilitating time in my life.


Exercise and proper nutrition has helped shape who I am today and gave me the confidence to move forward no matter what I am going through. I couldn't have accomplished this happier self without molding my mindset through positive thinking and meditation. Mindset has improved my life drastically and i've realized that this is the foundation to recovery.


My functional training program will help build the essentials needed to permanently change your life and help you better cope with the effects of depression and anxiety. I specialize in people working with depression and anxiety, but I also provide services for the more experienced weight lifter.

Online Personal Training

My program offers the three essentials for working through the pain of depression and anxiety. This includes a physical exercise, nutrition, and mindset routine that I'll develop for you from the information that I recieve during our calls.

  • Mindset through positive thinking and meditation. Meditation is better than medication!
  • A customized nutrition plan or nutrition template designed for your needs
  • A daily/weekly exercise routine that will improve your mental health and help with recovery.

Here at PM VIP Fitness we believe in using mindset, exercise and nutrition to lead a happier life!

What To Expect On The Calls...

1

Discovery Call

You'll schedule a 10 min discovery call where we'll get to know one another and determine what your needs and goals are. This gives you a chance to tell me a little about what you're going through and why you'd like to begin my program.

2

Info Call

Then there will be an info call that will last about 45-60 minutes. We'll discuss how depression and anxiety is affecting your life and we'll go over the details of my program and how it will help. I'll use this information to design a routine that is best for you.

3

Follow Up Calls

Then, once you've signed up here at PM VIP Fitness, I'll give you my mindset, nutrition, and exercise routines to follow. It's time to begin your journey to a life without depression and anxiety and I'll be there to guide you through the process.
